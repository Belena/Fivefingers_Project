Shipping Costs

Delivery is currently Free of Charge to Irish addresses. We deliver your goods within one week in Ireland. If we are unable to deliver within this timeframe you will be notified by email detailing the expected delivery date and alternative products. You may then decide to wait for your original product to be delivered, change your order to one of the available alternatives of to cancel your order.

Returns

We offer a flexible returns policy and are happy to accept items back for a refund or exchange if they don’t fit, you’ve ordered the wrong things, the items are damaged or faulty upon receipt or you simply don’t like what you’ve ordered.Email us indicating your intention to return a product, outlining any fault with the product, the size or style you wish in your exchange, or simply that you want a refund.

  • Goods must be returned within 14 days of receipt.
  • Goods must be adequate insurance during return shipping.
  • Goods must be in original packaging.
  • Goods must not be fitted or used.
  • Goods must be accompanied with a Completed Returns Form, received from Barefoot.ie

Refunds are normally processed within a week from the date we receive your return and by the same method you used for your original payment.
Any monies refunded are for the cost of the goods only. Postage is non-refundable.
Faulty product will receive a full refund provided a proof of purchase is provided
Products returned for exchange to different style of size will have a €10 delivery charge applied for the return shipping costs.
If the product returned is not in fully resalable condition or the packaging is damaged, we reserve the right to refuse a refund on the item, or deduct up to 20% of the original selling price from the refund amount. This does not affect your statutory rights.

Cancellations

You have the legal right to cancel your order within 7 working days of receiving the goods. If you wish to cancel using this right, you need to inform us in writing within 7 working days of receiving the goods. You must take reasonable care of the goods and immediately return them to us in the normal way.
